What is the Republic?

Plato’s Republic is one of the most renowned pieces of Greek philosophy and remains to be one of the most instructive ones of the Socratic dialogues. It delves into the what justice means, in the precise sense. It is comprised of 10 chapters, called “books” by Plato. In this post I will try delivering the essence of what goes down in Book 1 of the Republic.
